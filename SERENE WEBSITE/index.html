Covid-19 Safety Protocols

Our massage sofas will be fully disinfected by Saniter Wipes in full view of the customer before they are seated.

We will use the largest Coway air purifier model from Korea – able to filter out 99.9% of all particles and viruses with its triple medical grade HEPA filter.

Medical grade N95 masks and eye shields will always be worn by our therapists.

Fully Vaccinated Therapists
And Weekly Tests

Our therapists will have their third booster shots as soon as they are eligible, and weekly Covid tests will be performed.

Strict Hand Sanitizing
Procedures

OneMed Aseptic Gel will be available at the reception and every seat. Our therapists will fully sanitize their hands before and after every session in full view of the customer.
